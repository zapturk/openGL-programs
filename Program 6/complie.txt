controls:
+: Zoom In
-: Zoom Out
L: Rotate Y+
K: Rotate Y-
>: Rotate X+
<: Rotate X-
b: bounding box
d: scale down
D: scale up
e: shine up
E: shine down
f: fill/wireframe
s: smooth/flat
r: reset
ESC: Exit
